About Us

PVR Co-living & Guestin offers a modern, community-oriented stay in the heart of Gachibowli, Hyderabad. Designed with the needs of students, working professionals, and travelers in mind, we deliver high-quality, affordable accommodation that encourages connection, productivity, and comfort.

Our mission is to provide a space where people not only stay but thrive — whether you’re working remotely, studying for exams, or exploring Hyderabad. Our furnished rooms, shared workspaces, and event-driven environment foster both privacy and social interaction.

We believe living should be easy. That’s why we offer flexible contracts, multiple payment options, and around-the-clock support. Come home to a secure, convenient, and inspiring space with PVR Co-living & Guestin.

At PVR, every element is designed to make life easier: seamless booking, prompt maintenance, inclusive utilities, and vibrant community events ensure that you not only find a place to stay but also a place to belong. Located near major tech hubs, educational institutions, and shopping centers, we put the city at your doorstep.

Our Services

We provide short-term and long-term stays, with both private and shared rooms available. Each unit is equipped with high-speed WiFi, clean bedding, desks, and access to community kitchens and lounges.

Daily housekeeping, filtered drinking water, and hot showers ensure comfort, while social events and networking meetups enrich your living experience. We cater to all your basic and lifestyle needs under one roof, eliminating the hassle of managing utilities or chores.

We also offer assistance with moving in, on-site staff for maintenance, laundry services, and common areas for relaxation or collaboration. Flexible leases make it easy to stay as long as you like, whether for a few nights or many months. Our app-based booking system makes registration and service requests a breeze.

PVR Co-living also supports a strong community vibe, hosting workshops, meetups, and celebrations. Our team ensures cleanliness, security, and seamless support — your peace of mind is our priority.
